String Case Converter

Convert text between different cases: camelCase, snake_case, kebab-case, PascalCase, and more.

FAQ

What cases are supported?

This tool supports camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, kebab-case, CONSTANT_CASE, Sentence case, Title Case, and lowercase.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser.
