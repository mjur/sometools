HTML Entity Encoder/Decoder

Encode and decode HTML entities. Convert special characters to/from HTML entities locally in your browser.

FAQ

What are HTML entities?

HTML entities are codes used to represent special characters in HTML. For example, < becomes &lt; and & becomes &amp;.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser.
