FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using AI models.

How do the AI models work?

The AI models are downloaded once and cached in your browser. They run entirely on your device using WebGL/WebGPU acceleration.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, WebP, and other common image formats. The models work best with photos and realistic images.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on the image size and model. Typically 5-30 seconds for balanced quality settings.

Can I clear the model cache?

Yes, you can clear cached models from your browser's storage settings, or we'll provide a cache management option soon.
