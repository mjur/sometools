FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF formats.

How do I blur with the brush?

Click and drag on the image to paint blur effects in real-time. The brush applies blur as you paint, giving you immediate visual feedback. Adjust brush size and blur intensity to get the effect you want.

What is blur intensity?

Blur intensity controls how strong the blur effect is. Lower values (1-10) create a subtle blur, while higher values (30-50) create a very strong blur effect. Adjust based on your needs.

What is brush size?

Brush size controls the diameter of the blur brush. Smaller brushes (10-30) are good for detailed work, while larger brushes (100-200) are good for blurring large areas quickly.

Can I blur the entire image at once?

Yes, check the "Blur Entire Image" checkbox and then click anywhere on the image to apply blur to the whole image at once.
