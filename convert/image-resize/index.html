FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF formats.

How does aspect ratio preservation work?

When enabled, the tool automatically calculates the other dimension to maintain the original aspect ratio. You only need to specify width or height.
