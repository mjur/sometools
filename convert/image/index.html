FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, WebP, SVG, and ICO formats. You can convert between these formats.

Can I convert SVG to raster formats?

Yes, you can convert SVG to PNG, JPEG, or WebP. The SVG will be rendered at the specified resolution.

What is ICO format?

ICO is a container format for icons that can contain multiple sizes. When converting to ICO, you can select which sizes to include.

Can I adjust the quality of JPEG/WebP output?

Yes, use the quality sliders to adjust compression. Higher quality means larger file size but better image quality.
