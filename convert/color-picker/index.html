FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser.

How does color extraction work?

The tool analyzes the image pixels and extracts the most prominent colors, displaying them in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats.

Can I click on the image to pick a color?

Yes, click anywhere on the image preview to get the exact color at that point.
