FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP image formats.

Can I customize the PDF page size?

Yes, you can choose from A4, Letter, or custom page sizes, and set portrait or landscape orientation.

What happens if my image is larger than the page?

If "Fit image to page" is enabled, the image will be scaled to fit within the page margins. Otherwise, the image will be placed at its original size, which may extend beyond the page.
