FAQ

Is my image sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using image processing techniques. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF formats. The output is always PNG with transparency.

How accurate is the background removal?

The tool uses color-based and edge detection techniques. Works best with solid backgrounds or images where the background color is distinct from the subject. For best results, use the Magic Wand tool and click on the background area.

How do I use the Magic Wand tool?

Select "Magic Wand" from the method dropdown, then click on any part of the background you want to remove. The tool will select all similar pixels based on the tolerance setting.

What is tolerance?

Tolerance controls how similar colors need to be to be removed. Lower values (10-20) remove only very similar colors. Higher values (50-100) remove a wider range of similar colors. Adjust based on your image.
