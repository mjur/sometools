FAQ

What formats are supported?

ISO 8601, RFC 2822 (email-style), and common YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm patterns, with or without explicit time zones. Simple phrases like now or tomorrow 14:00 are also supported.

Which time zones can I convert to?

We show the parsed time in UTC, your browser's local zone, and several common IANA time zones like America/Los_Angeles, America/New_York, Europe/Berlin, Asia/Tokyo, and Australia/Sydney.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All parsing and conversion happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript.
