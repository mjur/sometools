FAQ

What is WCAG?

WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) provides standards for web accessibility. Level AA requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. Level AAA requires 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser.
