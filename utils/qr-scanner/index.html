FAQ

How does webcam scanning work?

Click "Start Camera" to access your webcam. Point it at a QR code and it will automatically scan and decode the content.

Can I scan from an image file?

Yes, select "Upload Image" mode and choose an image file containing a QR code.

Is my camera data sent to a server?

No. All scanning happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
