FAQ

      

        What diff granularity options are available?
        
You can choose to compare texts at the line, word, or character level.

      

      

        Can I ignore whitespace?
        
Yes, enable the 'Ignore whitespace' option to compare texts without considering whitespace differences.

      

      

        Can I export the diff?
        
Yes, use the 'Copy Diff' button to copy the unified diff format to your clipboard.
